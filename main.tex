% Interests
I define art broadly as illusions beyond the creative medium,
intending to resonate or shock and invoke emotions and thoughts.
My high-level objective is the computational formalization of the emotional side of humanity -
Can machines \textit{create} publicly-accepted art? \par \vspace{0.375em}

Toward this end, I’m currently interested in sequence or image generation by
applying Natural Language Processing, Representation Learning, and Computer Vision
to understand and create works that resemble art and to foster accessible human art generation.
I hope the general public can produce and interact with pleasant pieces with simple inputs. \par
