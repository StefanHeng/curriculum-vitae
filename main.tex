% Interests
I define art broadly as illusions beyond the creative medium,
intending to resonate or shock and invoke emotions and thoughts.
My high-level objective is the computational formalization of the emotional side of humanity -
Can machines \textit{create} publicly-accepted art?
% \par \vspace{0.375em}
Toward this end, I’m currently interested in sequence or image generation or manipulation by
applying Natural Language Processing, Representation Learning, and Computer Vision to
1) understand and create works that resemble art and 2) foster accessible human art generation.
I hope to lower the barrier for everyone to
create and curate appealing pieces with simple inputs. \par
