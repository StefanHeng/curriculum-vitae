% Interests
Art can be defined broadly as illusions as the audiences' sensations and meanings arise
from the original artistic medium.
My high-level goal is to develop computational tools to understand and create artworks that helps
fostering accessible art generation. I hope that the general public can produce and interact with
pleasant pieces from simple inputs.
Toward this end, I'm currently interested in image or sequence generation through
Natural Language Processing, Representation Learning and Computer Vision. \par
